**泥垢](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/泥垢)kau-koih630

  • Scurf ; grime.

  • háng sói-êk, cêk sin cĕng-cĕng kau-koih;

  • hía kau-koih m̄ tîeh chìu cheng-khih?

    Why do you not scour off all the scurf?

**麥**
- Wheat.

- síe bêh;

  common wheat.

- tōa bêh;

  barley.

- saⁿ-kak bêh;

  buckwheat.

- bêh-sūi;

  an ear of wheat.

- bêh-kó̤;

  wheat stubble.

- bêh-kó̤-cháu bō̤;

  a hat made of wheat straw.

- bêh pho;

  bran.

- bêh hún;

  flour made from parched wheat.

- bêh chòi;

  coarse wheaten flour.

- bêh kúe;

  wheaten cakes.

- bêh luah;

  strips of dried paste.

- kuah bêh;

  to reap wheat.

- oi bêh;

  to grind wheat.

- phah bêh;

  sut bêh; to thresh wheat.

- bêh gê;

  wheat sprouts, used in medicine.

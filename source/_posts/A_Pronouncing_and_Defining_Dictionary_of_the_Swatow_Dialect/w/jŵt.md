**悅**
- Gratified; contented; delighted; to please.

- i kâi sim hí-jŵt;

  he is delighted.

- tó lăi put-jŵt;

  displeased in his heart.

- i thiaⁿ tîeh m̄-jŵt;

  when he heard it he was displeased.

- khó̤-íⁿ jŵt mâk;

  rather pleasing to the eye.

- thóiⁿ tîeh jŵt nâng kâi mâk;

  the sight of it gladdens the eye.

**關**

To close a door; to shut.

- kueⁿ mn̂g;

  shut the door.

- khṳ̀ kàu, i kâi mn̂g kueⁿ miⁿ-miⁿ;

  when I got there his door was tightly closed.

- mn̂g kueⁿ hó̤;

  the house is closed for the night.

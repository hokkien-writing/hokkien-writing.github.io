**呼**
- To send the breath through the nose.

- hngk phīⁿ;

  to blow the nose.

- phīⁿ khang huang hngk chut hngk jîp;

  to exhale and inhale the air.

- phīⁿ khang hngk khṳ̀ cêk kâi m̄ thong;

  I cannot breath through one of my nostrils.

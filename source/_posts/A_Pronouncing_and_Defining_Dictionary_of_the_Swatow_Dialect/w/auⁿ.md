**呶**
- Clamorous vociferation.

- auⁿ-auⁿ jíang;

  noisy bickering.

- i auⁿ-auⁿ jíang sĭ cò̤-nî?

  What are they clamoring for?

- auⁿ-auⁿ-kìe;

  the sound of many voices as in strife.

- tàⁿ tîeh, i auⁿ-auⁿ chang;

  if you say anything about it he makes a great fuss.

**捱**
- To suffer; to endure hardness.

- i mîaⁿ-ke ngâih căi;

  he is very plucky.

- kheh-nâng hùe īu ŏi ngâih;

  the Hakka people have endurance.

- cí kâi khó pât nâng ngâih m̄ khṳ̀;

  others could not bear such hardship.

- khak-khó ngâih;

  patiently bear with.

- khuaⁿ-khuaⁿ ngâih;

  put up with it quietly.

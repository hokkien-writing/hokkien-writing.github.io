**𠔠**
- To stew; to boil in water with a noise.

- khûh kàu mîⁿ-mîⁿ;

  boil it soft.

- khieh khṳ̀ huang-lô khûh kàu lok-lok thúe-thúe;

  take it to the portable furnace and stew it to a porridge.

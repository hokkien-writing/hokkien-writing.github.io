**唾**
- To spit.

- phùi nŭaⁿ;

  to expectorate.

- phùi thâm, phùi hueh;

  to spit up phlegm or blood.

- thâm-nŭaⁿ màiⁿ phùi cêk koiⁿ;

  do not spit over everything.

- i kâi thâm phùi chut lâi tòa hueh tíam;

  there are specks of blood in the phlegm which he raises.

- phùi-phùi-kìe;

  keeps spitting.

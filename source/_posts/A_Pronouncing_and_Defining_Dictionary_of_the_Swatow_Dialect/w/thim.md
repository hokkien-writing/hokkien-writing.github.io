**沉**
- To put under water; to sink a thing; weighty; heavy.

- thīm-hieⁿ;

  garu-wood, or lignaloes.

- thīm pàng cúi tói cìm;

  sink it to the bottom and let it soak.

- thīm lô̤h hái;

  sink it in thesea.

- phŏ̤ tîeh thīm-thīm tăng;

  he is very heavy to carry.

- im-thīm ak;

  hidden wickedness.

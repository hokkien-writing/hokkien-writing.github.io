**啜**
- To drink or sip with a noise.

- chueh thng;

  to eat soup noisily.

- phîn-khîong nâng cîah mûeⁿ chueh ám;

  the poor eat congee and sip the broth.

- chueh m̄ lô̤h;

  cannot sup it.

- chueh tîeh tîam căi;

  sipped and found it very sweet.

**泣**
- To weep silently, to sob, to catch the breath as in sobbing.

- i choih mih sṳ̄?

  What is she crying about?

- khàu kàu choih;

  wept so violently as to sob.

- khàu hiah hŵn-lío tó̤ choih;

  after he had stopped crying he still continued to sob.

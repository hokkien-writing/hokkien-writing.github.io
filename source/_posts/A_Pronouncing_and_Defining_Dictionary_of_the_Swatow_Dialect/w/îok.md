**慾**
- Appetite; concupiscence.

- sṳ iok;

  selfish ends.

- chêng îok;

  passions.

- îok-húe sĕng;

  lust is roused.

- îok sim;

  a lacivious mind.

- kúaⁿ îok;

  weak desires.

- i cí kâi pēⁿ, sĭ sek îok thài kùe tì kâi;

  this disease arises from unrestrained passions.

- pù-kùi to̤ îm-îok;

  the rich are very lustful.

- co̤h îok;

  to control passion.

- mûeh îok;

  objects of desire.

**肓**
- To rear; to nurture; to bring up.

- îok-eng-tn̂g;

  a foundling hospital.

- íang-îok;

  to nurture.

- seⁿ îok;

  bear and rear.

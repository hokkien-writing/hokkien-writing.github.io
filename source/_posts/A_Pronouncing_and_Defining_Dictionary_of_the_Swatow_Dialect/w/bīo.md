**妙**
- Wonderful; subtle; mysterious.

- bīo căi;

  very wonderful.

- sĭm bīo;

  very subtle.

- kèng bīo;

  more difficult to fathom.

- kêk bīo;

  extremely abstruse.

- àu-bīo;

  mysterious.

- khá bīo;

  ingenious.

- bīo huap;

  an admirable method.

- bīo chíu;

  a skilled artist or physician.

- bīo kòi;

  a capital scheme.

- khî bīo;

  surprisingly clever.

- bīo sĭeⁿ;

  subtle thought.

- bīo pín;

  a fine thing.

- bīo îeh;

  a wonderful remedy.

- sîn bīo;

  supernatural.

- sîn bīo bô̤ pí;

  incomparably perfect.

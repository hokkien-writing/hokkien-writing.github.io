- To address.

- kiok-kiong;

  to bow.

- kiok-kiong kìⁿ lói;

  the etiquette of salutation.

- i cē kìⁿ tîeh kiok-kiong kiok-kiong;

  as soon as he saw him he bowed repeatedly.

**暢**
- The inner qualities developing.

- mêⁿ-hng lài thìang ím;

  come and take a social glass in the evening.

- ūe tàⁿ lâi thìang-tâk;

  it was said in a very perspicuous style.

- thìang-khùai căi;

  very delightsome or pleasurable.

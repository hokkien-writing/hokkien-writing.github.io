**逆**
- To resist; to encounter; contrary; refractory.

- ngêk lâu;

  go against the tide.

- ngêk huang;

  an opposing wind.

- mông hẃn, pw̆n ngêk;

  to plot rebellion.

- ngó-ngêk;

  pig-headed; obstinate.

- ngêk tîeh i kâi ì;

  opposed his wishes.

- ngêk tîeh i kâi sì;

  go against the grain, or against the current.

- cí kâi cèng sĭ ngêk cèng;

  this disease is an obstinate one.

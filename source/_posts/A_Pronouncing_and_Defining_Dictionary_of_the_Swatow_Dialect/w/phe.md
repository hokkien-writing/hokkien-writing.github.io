**帕**
- A kerchief; a veil; a stomacher; to gather up, as in an apron.

- chíu phè;

  a handkerchief.

- cia máng-phè;

  wear a veil.

- lô̤ phè;

  a lace veil.

- cang saⁿ-phè lâi phè;

  gather them up in the skirt of your tunic.

- pâk kâi âng tó-phè;

  wore a red stomacher.

**衸**
- Long robes.

- chēng kâi saⁿ, tn̂g-phĕ-phĕ;

  wore a very long tunic.

**剪**
- To trim off ; to clip.

- àiⁿ pīn tī kò̤ lîe?

  From what point shall I shear it off?

- khîeh khṳ̀ lîe cói cē;

  take it and trim it off even.

- lîe pêⁿ-pêⁿ tn̂g;

  clip them so as to make them of the same length.

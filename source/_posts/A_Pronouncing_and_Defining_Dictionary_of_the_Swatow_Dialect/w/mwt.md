- Least and last; insignificant; the end.

- pún mŵt;

  origin and end.

- mûiⁿ mŵt;

  atomic.

- mŵt căi;

  exceedingly small.

- lĭ-sek mŵt căi;

  the profit is scarcely any.

- mŵt-mŵt nē;

  very insignificant.

- sĭm sĭ mŵt thàng;

  earned hardly anything.

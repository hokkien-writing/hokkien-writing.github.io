**猫**
- A cat.

- tōa ciah ngio;

  a large cat.

- ngio ngāu-ngāu-kìe;

  the cats waul.

- ngio-ngio-kìe;

  to mew.

- ngio kio nío-chṳ̀ cò̤-pû ût;

  the cat and the rat sleeping together.

- ngio auⁿ-auⁿ-kìe;

  the cat is snarling.

**錨**
- Grapling irons.

- hái cûn to̤ sĭ ēng thih ngio cò̤ tèng;

  sea-going vessels generally use grappling irons as anchors.

**尿**
- Urine.

- pàng jīe;

  to urinate.

- jīe-pau;

  the bladder.

- jīe lô̤-lô̤, m̄ cheng;

  turbid urine.

- bŏi pàng jīe;

  retention of urine.

- chūa jīe; jīe chūa cêk mîn-chn̂g;

  to wet the bed.

**播**
- To disseminate; to strew; to scatter abroad; to sow.

- pùa-îang i kâi mîaⁿ;

  publish his name far and wide.

- thŵn-pùa;

  to promulgate.

**簸**
- A winnowing; to winnow.

- pùa bí;

  to winnow rice.

- thai pùa; to sift and winnow.

- pùa-ki;

  a winnowing basket.

- pùa tīo cho-khng;

  winnow away the chaff.

- khṳt huang éng pùa tîeh;

  splashed by wind and waves.

**撈**
- To grapple from a deep place; to dredge for; to drag for.

- căⁿ hṳ̂;

  to dredge for fish.

- căⁿ ô̤;

  to dredge for oysters.

- căⁿ sap;

  to dredge for the broken shells of which lime is made.

**平**
- Even; uniform.

- phêⁿ siaⁿ;

  the even tones.

- cĭeⁿ phêⁿ, ĕ phêⁿ;

  the high even and the low even tones.

- sĭ phêⁿ im a sĭ ceh im?

  Is it an even tone or is it one of the other three tones?

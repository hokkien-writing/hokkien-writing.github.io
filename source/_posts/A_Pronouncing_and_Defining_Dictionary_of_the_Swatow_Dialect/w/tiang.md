**誰**
- Who, whose, anyone.

- cía sĭ tîang kâi?

  Whose is this?

- tîang káⁿ cìeⁿ-seⁿ?

  Who would venture to do that?

- tîang m̄ cai?

  Who does not know that?

- tîang tàⁿ?

  Who said so?

- m̄ cai tìang kâi kíaⁿ;

  do not know whose child it is.

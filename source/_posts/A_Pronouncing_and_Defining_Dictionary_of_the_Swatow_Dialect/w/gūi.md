**䭳**

- a-gūi;

  asafetida, brought from Persia and Cashmere, and used for plaisters. It is also burnt as a deodorizer.

- a-gūi bô̤ cin, khiak ŭ cin;

  though asafetida is not real, there is such a thing as real asafetida.

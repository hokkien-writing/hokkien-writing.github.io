**綽**
- Many; liberal.

- chiak-hō̤;

  a nickname or pet name.

- i kâi chiak-hō̤ kìe-cò̤ sĭm-mûeh?

  What nickname has he?

- chiak-chiak ŭ ṳ̂;

  there is some to spare.

**雀**
- A sparrow.

- ìⁿ-chiak;

  a house sparrow.

- n̂g-chiak;

  a goldfinch.

- chiak téng;

  the bird crest, a peculiar shaped button worn by those who have taken a degree.

**妾**
- A concubine.

- cêk chi cêk chiap;

  a wife and a concubine.

- bói chiap;

  buy a concubine.

- síe chiap;

  my concubine.

- ài chiap;

  your concubine.

- thóng chiap khì chi;

  divorce the wife for love of the concubine.

**竊**
- To pilfer, to steal.

- thau-chiap;

  to steal.

- pĭ chiap;

  stolen.

- chiap-châk;

  a petty thief.

**玉**
- A stone fit for a lapidary; jade and fine quartz.

- pêh gêk;

  white jade.

- hui-chùi gêk;

  mottled jade.

- gêk khì;

  articles of jade or quartz.

- gêk pue, gêk chíu-hŵn, gêk pang-cí, gêk chíu-cak;

  jade cups, bracelets, thumb-rings, and finger-rings.

- gêk phùe-thâu;

  jade ornamentation.

- gêk hĭⁿ kau-thūi;

  jade ear drops.

- gêk ńng;

  your daughter.

- gêk jîn;

  a lovely girl.

- kim gêk;

  very lovely.

- gêk cîeh kŭ ûn;

  indiscriminate destruction.

- kim gêk căi;

  precious, admirable.

 

**獄**
- A prison; a dungeon.

- tī-gêk;

  the abode of the damned.

- lô̤-gêk;

  purgatory.

- lô̤h tī-gêk;

  go to hell.

- phah lô̤h tī-gêk;

  sent to hell.

- gêk-cut;

  a jailor.

- khui tī-gêk mn̂g;

  release from purgatory.

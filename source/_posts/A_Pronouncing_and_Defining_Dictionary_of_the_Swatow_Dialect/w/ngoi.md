**研**
- To triturate.

- ngói cò̤ bûah;

  grind it to powder.

- ngói lío cìaⁿ thai;

  triturate and then sift it.

- ngói cô̤;

  a triturating trough.

- ngói cô̤ lín;

  a wheel used in grinding in a grinding trough.

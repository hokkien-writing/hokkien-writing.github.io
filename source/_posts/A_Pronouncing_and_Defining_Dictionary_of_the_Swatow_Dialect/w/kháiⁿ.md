**𤿠**
- Blackness of the skin.

- i kâi mīn kháiⁿ-kháiⁿ-o;

  his face is very black.

- i sĭ seⁿ lâi kâi mīn khái-khái-o, a sĭ kô kâi mīn khái-khái-o?

  Is his face naturally black, or has he blackened it?

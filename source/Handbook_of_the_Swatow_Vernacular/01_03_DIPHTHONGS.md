### DIPHTHONGS.

In all the diphthongs each vowel is heard distinctly with its own proper sound.

ai	as	y	in	fly
au	„	ow	„	cow
oi	„	oy	„	boy
ou nearly	„	ou	„	four
ua	„	wa	„	war
ui	„	wee	„	weed

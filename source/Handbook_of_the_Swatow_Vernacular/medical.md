## Lesson XX. medical.

* 【先生】(Sin-seⁿ) A doctor.
* 【醫】(Ui) To cure.
* 【病】(Pēⁿ) To be ill; sickness.
* 【儂毋孬】(Nâng m̃-móⁿ) Unwell.
* 【破病】(Phùa-pēⁿ) To be very ill for a long period.
* 【着力】(Tíh-lát) To be very ill, almost dying.

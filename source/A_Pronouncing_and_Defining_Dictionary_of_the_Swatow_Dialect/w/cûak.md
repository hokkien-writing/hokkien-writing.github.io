**濁**
- Muddy; turbid; thick.

- siaⁿ-im cûak;

  has a hoarse voice.

- mīn sek cûak, m̄ cheng;

  his complexion is not clear.

- cuí thóiⁿ tîeh cûak, m̄ cheng-khih;

  the water does not appear to be clean.

- khì-bī cûak;

  a stuffy smell.

- khì-bī lông-cûak;

  a heavy mixed smell.

- hun cheng cûak;

  distinguish between the clean and the dirty.

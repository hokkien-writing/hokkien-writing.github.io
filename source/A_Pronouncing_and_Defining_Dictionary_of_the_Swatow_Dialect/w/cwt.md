**拙**
- Stupid.

- hṳ́ kâi cū sĭ úa kâi lăi cwt;

  that is my wife.

- cìeⁿ-seⁿ cìaⁿ hó̤ châng-cwt;

  then it is best to keep mum.

- úa kâi seⁿ-cieh sĭm sĭ ngô̤-cwt;

  I am naturally very stupid.

**訝**
- To wonder; to express surprise.

- thóiⁿ tîeh kiaⁿ-gîa;

  beheld it with amazement.

- cèng nâng thiaⁿ tîeh kâi-kâi kiaⁿ-gîa hăi-ĭ;

  when they heard it they were every one of them filled with wonder and surprise.

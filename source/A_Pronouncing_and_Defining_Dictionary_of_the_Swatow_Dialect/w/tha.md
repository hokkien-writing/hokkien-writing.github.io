**他**
- A personal pronoun, he, she, it, that, the other, another.

- tha nâng;

  the other person.

- màiⁿ kẃn tha;

  pay no attention to it.

- lṳ́ úa tha lâi sie kāng pun;

  let us, you, me and him, divide it among us.

- îu tha;

  let him alone.

- lîu kàu tha hng gūa lí;

  wandered away into other countries.

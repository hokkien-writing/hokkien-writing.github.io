**膪**
- Fat; like marrow; soft.

- pûi kàu thē-thē;

  stout and fat.

- cêk sin nêk thē-thē;

  fat in flesh.

- kâi tó thē-thē;

  pursy.

**䖳**
- The sea-blubber or jelly-fish.

- bói kò̤ thē lâi iam;

  buy a slice of jelly-fish and dress it with sugar and vinegar.

- thē-phûe;

  the flesh of the jelly-fish.

**麼**
- An interrogative particle; a sort; what; whatever.

- lṳ́ tó̤ cò̤ mih sṳ̄?

  What are you doing?

- i sĭ sĭm-mih nâng?

  Who is he?

- hṳ́ kâi sĭ sĭm-mih mûeh?

  What is that?

- lṳ́ kìe-cò̤ sĭm-mih mîaⁿ?

  What is your name.

- bô̤ mih hó̤;

  not particularly good.

- ŭ mih sṳ̄ mē?

  Is anything the matter?

- i bó̤ mih cîⁿ;

  he has not much cash.

- mih mûeh i to àiⁿ;

  whatever it be he wants it.

- cía sĭ mih ūe?

  What sort of talk is this?

- bô̤ sĭm-mih ngía;

  not very handsome.

- i sin cīeⁿ bô̤ mih sóng-khùai;

  he is not very well.

- mih kĭaⁿ ŏi;

  can do any sort of thing.

**耨**
- To weed; to clear grounds of grass.

- thóaⁿ cháu;

  pull up the weeds.

- cháu thóaⁿ cheng-khih lío;

  the weeds are all pulled.

**檀**
- Tough hard wood, resembling rosewood.

- thôaⁿ-hieⁿ;

  sandal wood.

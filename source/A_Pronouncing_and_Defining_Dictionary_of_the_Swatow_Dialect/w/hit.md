**曳**
- To swing or dangle.

- hit kha;

  to swing the feet.

- ki búe pŵt-pŵt hit;

  his tail keeps wagging.

- chíu hit-a-hit;

  the hands swinging carelessly.

- màiⁿ hit ka-lâuh khṳ̀;

  do not drop it from your hands as you swing them.

- kîaⁿ lō chíu màiⁿ hit khah khui;

  in walking do not swing your hands too much.

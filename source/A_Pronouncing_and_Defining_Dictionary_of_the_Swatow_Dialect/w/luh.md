**呷**
- To gulp down.

- luh ceng tê;

  gulped down a cup of tea.

- cêh úaⁿ luh cò̤ nŏ̤ saⁿ chùi cū ŵn;

  take down the bowlful at two or three gulps.

- luh khah tōa chùi cho̤h âu;

  choked by gulping too big mouthfuls.
